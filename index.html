Luck of the Roll

To start your gambling adventure click on the dice and see your initial luck. To begin gambling your money away, all you have to do is type in the selected bet you want start with then press enter when ready.
If you get a sum of 100 or higher then you get x10 your bet.
If you get a sum of 75 to 99 then get x2 your bet.
If you get a sum of lower than 80 then you lose the bet.
